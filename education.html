Education Spending Compared to GDP(%) 2011-2016


  • Brazil (as a developing country) has the remarkable highest 6% share of GDP in education, also showing a trend of increasing
  • No obvious trend for other countries
  • All developed countries show similar high education spending in %
  • China has the lowest share of GDP in education

Overall Education Spending vs Military (2011-2016 Average)

  • China has higher military spending than education
  • US and China are the higher end of education/militray spending ratio

Per capita Education Spending vs Per capita GDP (2011-2016 Average)

  • Positive correlate observed
  • Developed countries have similar high education spending per person
  • From the slope, we can see that Brazil still spend the highest ratio of per person education compared to their per person GDP

Education Spending Growth in Absolute (2011-2016)

  • Most countries have no obvious trend for education spending
  • US & China increased the education spending

Education Spending Growth in Percentage (2011-2016)

  • China and India showed obvious growth in education spending in percentage
  • China showed fastest growth in percentage
