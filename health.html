Health Spending Compared to GDP(%) 2011-2016


  • US is far exceeding other countries in health spending 17% share of GDP
  • Brazil (as a developing country) has high spending share of GDP in healthcare, comparable to other developed countries
  • All developed countries show similar high healthcare spending compared to GDP
  • Healthcare spending remains consistent for all countries

Overall Healthcare Spending vs Military (2011-2016 Average)


  • Healthcare represents the largest expense for all countries!

Per capita Healthcare Spending vs Per capita GDP (2011-2016 Average)


  • Developed countries have similar high healthcare spending per person
  • Brazil spend more in healthcare than other developing countries

Healthcare Spending Growth in Absolute (2011-2016)


  • No obvious trend observed for healthcarespending
  • US & China increased the healthcare spending
  • US has the fastest growth in absolute value

Healthcare Spending Growth in Percentage (2011-2016)


  • No huge percentage change for most of countries
  • China, US and India showed consitent increase
  • China showed fastest growth in percentage
